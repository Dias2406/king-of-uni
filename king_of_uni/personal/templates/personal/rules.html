Aim of the Game:

Teams compete over territories around campus; the team with the most players in a territory will gain control over that
territory. Teams earn points for controlling a territory, the more territory a team control the more points that team
will earn. If a team gains complete control over every territory in the University they will be crowned the kings of
University and recieve the mystery prize.

Information:

  • Zones will be available to capture by Gamekeepers; players can look at the map on the main menu to see where these
    capture points are.
  • When a player is near a capture point they can use the capture button on the home screen to start caturing the zone.
  • Players and teams compete to earn the highest number of points by capturing territories/areas around campus.
  • These territories are marked on the map after login.
  • Once inside a territory, the player can use the capture territory button to capture it and gain points.
  • Each player may only capture the same point once per day.
  • At the end of each day, the team which has captured a territory the most times are awarded additional points.
  • Point totals and game progress are tracked and compared via the leaderboard.
