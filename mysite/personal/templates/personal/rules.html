Aim of the Game:

Teams compete over territories around campus; players are able to go up to building marked on the user home page map and
capture it. Teams earn points for succesfully capturing a building and earn streaks the longer a team can keep hold of
the building. If a team gains complete control over every territory in the University they will be crowned the kings of
University.

Information:

  • Zones will be available to capture by Gamekeepers; players can look at the map on the main menu to see where these
    capture points are.
  • When a player wants to a capture a building they can use the check my location button in the territories screen
  • If the player is close enough the capture button will become available to press and the player can capture the building
  • Each building has a cooldown and can't be recaptured until the cooldown expires.
  • Players can check how long is left on a cooldown by trying to capture a territory.
  • Teams can earn streaks to earn high score bonuses by recapturing territories they hold at the end of a cooldown.
  • Point totals and game progress are tracked and compared via the leaderboard.
  • Players can check which team holds which buildings by clicking on the building markers on the map in the home screen
